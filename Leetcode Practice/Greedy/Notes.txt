- Greedy Choose Best Option avilable at moment
- Greedy doesnt worry whether current best optimal will bring overall optimal result or not
- Greedy never reverse a result if choise is wrong
- Greedy always Goes for local best choice to produce global best choice

We can use Greedy Approch to Problem if problem has following property
1. Greedy Choice Property = If you can choose best option at given step without re-considering previous step
2. Optimal SubStructure = if local optimal Solution brings you to global optimal
